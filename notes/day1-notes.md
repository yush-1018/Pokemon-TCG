# Day 1 Notes

## Game Rules
- Turn-based strategy game
- Each player has 60 cards
- 6 Prize Cards
- One Active Pokémon
- Up to 5 Benched Pokémon

## Turn Flow

Draw Card

↓

Attach Energy

↓

Play Trainer

↓

Evolve Pokémon

↓

Attack

↓

End Turn

## Card Types

- Pokémon
- Energy
- Trainer

## Win Conditions

- Collect all Prize Cards
- Opponent has no Pokémon
- Opponent cannot draw
